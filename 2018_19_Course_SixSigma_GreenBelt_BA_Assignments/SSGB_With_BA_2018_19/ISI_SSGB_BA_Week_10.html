2. Compute and plot the 1, 3, and 5 year moving average for the personal consumption expenditures and To compute moving averages on our data we can leverage the rollmean function from the zoo package

savings <- economics %>%
  select(date, srate = pce) %>%
  mutate(srate_ma01 = rollmean(srate, k = 13, fill = NA),
         srate_ma03 = rollmean(srate, k = 37, fill = NA),
         srate_ma05 = rollmean(srate, k = 61, fill = NA))

3. Compute the mean square error of these moving averages.

savings %>%
  gather(metric, value, srate_ma01:srate_ma05) %>%
  group_by(metric) %>%
  summarise(MSE = mean((srate - value)^2, na.rm = TRUE),
            MAPE = mean(abs((srate - value)/srate), na.rm = TRUE))
